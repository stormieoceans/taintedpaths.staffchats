Writer Prompts;
  • Your Warrior OC comes across a character from the canon series! What would happen and how would they interact?
  • Your character comes across an aeroplane for the first time, how would they react?
  • Your villain character tries to be a good guy for a week, do they fail or succeed?
  • Write about your character finding a pile of autumn leaves on a fall morning.
  • Write a monologue/poem based on your character's theme song.
  • Write a story about your character observing someone else; from 2nd Person POV.
  • “Love is like a plant. It has to be watered and nurtured and eventually it’ll grow and become stronger and stronger.”
“Can our love be the plant that eats bugs and stuff? That one’s really cool.” “… sure.”
  • Describe your character as if they were a landscape!
  • Your character is really nervous to ask another for directions to a place and is desperately trying to gather up the courage to do so.
  • Your character and a group of friends have a dance battle in the school's parking lot.
  • Your character gets a polaroid camera, what do they take photos of?
  • Write a short story about your character waking up in a flower forest.
  • Write a short story about your character waking up in a rustic cottage.
  • Two characters who absolutely despise each other after years of conflict are forced to work together to save something dear to the both of them.
  • Write a twisting tale about your character spinning a white lie into a web of lies.
  • Your character watches a scary movie and somehow end up in a trash can after trying to chase ghosts away.
  • What is your favourite drink, and why? Describe the first time you had that drink.
  • Your character comforts another after a terrible birthday celebration.
  • Your character attempts a heist and fails, miserably.
  • What's your favourite sport to play, or one you enjoy watching? What made you like the sport so much?
  • Write a poem about something on your desk.

Betrayal Welcome friends! The prompt we'll be using today is Betrayal. Betrayal is nearly always different depending on the situation. Though it has the same underlining description. Betrayal: The act of breaking one's trust. We see betrayal in many ways. Maybe your younger sibling ate the last pop-tart, knowing you were saving it to eat before your piano lessons and left the empty box in the pantry for you to discover, or maybe one of your Tainted-Paths characters betrayed someone. Whatever the situation might be, we want to know! Reminder(s): Don't talk in prompt creation. We understand you might be curious or want to compliment them, but there are other chats you can do that in. Your character doesn't have to be limited to Tainted Paths, it's up to you. Writers: Give us a story where your character finds out they've just been betrayed, or when someone finds out your character betrayed them. Artist: Make a scene from an Oc's past where your character is reacting to a betrayal, or an Oc's revealing they were the culprit behind the betrayal. Have fun, and more importantly, let your imagination run wild. (edited)
